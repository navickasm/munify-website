Schedule

MUNIFY takes place over a single day, striking the balance between an enriching experience and a reasonable commitment for middle school students.

  • 8:00 - 8:45

    Opening Ceremonies

    Features a keynote speaker and an engaging activity.

  • 9:00 - 11:30

    Committee Session I

  • 11:30 - 12:30

    Lunch

  • 12:30 - 2:30

    Committee Session II

  • 2:30 - 3:00

    Break

    Delegates may choose to receive feedback from Dais Staff at this time.

  • 3:00 - 4:45

    Committee Session III

  • 4:45 - 6:00

    Dinner

  • 6:00 - 7:00

    Committee Session IV

  • 7:15 - 8:00

    Closing Ceremonies

    Awards will be presented during Closing Ceremonies. May end earlier.

Sponsors may determine if students can leave the venue for meals, with or without accompaniment.

The Model United Nations Initiative for Youth (MUNIFY) is a Model United Nations conference designed specifically for middle schoolers to grow as speakers, communicators, writers, and readers in an engaging and age-appropriate manner hosted in the Chicagoland area.
